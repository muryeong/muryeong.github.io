🔥 Free Fire Newbie Corner

Beginner guide for players starting from zero.

What is Free Fire?

Free Fire is a battle royale game where 50 players land on an island and fight until only one remains. You collect weapons, survive the shrinking zone, and try to become the last player alive.

Basic Controls

  • Move using the left joystick
  • Aim and shoot using the right side
  • Pick up weapons and items
  • Use medkits to heal
Tip: Practice movement and aiming in training mode first.

Beginner Tips

  • Land in quieter areas to survive longer
  • Always pick up armor and helmet
  • Stay inside the safe zone
  • Don't rush fights until you get good gear

Weapons for Beginners

Some beginner-friendly weapons:

  • M1014 – powerful shotgun
  • UMP – easy SMG
  • AK – strong rifle
